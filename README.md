## Introduction

Speed Shopping is a web-based game-simulator of an online store that integrates webcam eye tracking (WebGazer.js) 
and facial emotion recognition (Face API) 
to study the influence of banner layout patterns on user cognitive load and emotional response.

The project was completed during the preparation of Semyon S. Petrov's research work 
at SPbPU Institute of Computer Science and Cybersecurity (SPbPU ICSC).

## Instruction

1. Open `game.html` in a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
2. Allow camera access.
3. Follow the calibration instructions: click on each red dot 5 times until it turns yellow.
4. Complete 5 shopping tasks – each task shows a banner with a different layout pattern (classic, multi, shifted, camouflage, dark).
5. Close the banner by finding the close button (cross).
6. After all tasks, download a ZIP archive with a JSON report and heatmaps.
